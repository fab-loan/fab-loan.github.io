L'Oréal - Forecasting Customer Spending

Data Visualisation – May 2024

Keywords: XGBoost – Random Forest – CLV - Logistic Regression – Linear Regression

  • By forecasting future spending patterns, L'Oréal Dermatological Beauty can identify high and low-value customers, optimising resource allocation for targeted campaigns. The analysis includes the impact of lagged spending, skin concern segmentation, and Customer Lifetime Value (CLV), all of which highlight opportunities for cross-selling, upselling, and retention strategies.
  • Limitations: small dataset and a lack of demographic details, which would enhance model accuracy

Key Research Questions:

  • Which skin concern segments are likely to generate the highest value in the next six months?
  • What trends in lagged spending and product purchases are the strongest predictors of total transaction value?
  • Which product categories are most popular among high-value customers, and how can this guide targeted marketing efforts?
  • How does the distribution of Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) across skin concern segments inform retention and upselling strategies?
  • Which consumers are likely to have future low or high transaction values?
